# Workflows

Step-by-step research protocols rendered as check-off step cards.

![Workflows](images/workflows.png)

## Protocols
- **Check-off steps** — track your progress through a protocol as step cards
- **Markdown format** — protocols are a simple markdown checklist dialect
- **Live-preview editor** — edit and preview side by side

## Built-in templates
Seven Canada-first templates for classic CADC/CANFAR research:
- Archival imaging reconnaissance
- Variable-star photometry
- Cube kinematics
- Stellar spectroscopy
- Catalogue cross-matching
- Proposal due diligence
- Batch reprocessing

## Storage & sharing
- **Local** — stored on your machine with per-protocol progress tracking
- **VOSpace** — publish and share protocols with your team
